Chatbot Script 01

Welcome & Routing Bot

Deploy on: Website homepage or help centre entrance. Purpose: Greet visitors and route them to the right resource.

How can I help? • Track my order / access my account • Get help with [PRODUCT] • Request a refund or change [route to relevant script based on selection]" --- IF "I have a question" --- BOT: "Fire away! Type your question and I'll do my best to answer, or connect you with someone who can." [Route to FAQ search or human handoff after 2 bot exchanges] --- HUMAN HANDOFF TRIGGER --- If bot cannot resolve after 2 exchanges: BOT: "Let me get a real human to help with this. 💡 Deploy tip: Add this bot to every page. A 2-second greeting beats making visitors hunt for contact info. Response time under 5 seconds = 3× higher engagement.

Blueprint 01

Automated Lead Nurture System

Time to set up: 3-4 hours | Time saved: 5-8 hours/week | Tools: Make.com (or Zapier) + email platform + AI

WHAT IT DOES: Automatically qualifies new leads, sends personalised email sequences, and notifies you only when a lead is "hot" (high engagement). TOOLS NEEDED: - Lead capture: your website form + [Typeform / JotForm] - Automation: Make.com (free tier works) or Zapier - Email: Mailchimp / ConvertKit / ActiveCampaign - AI: OpenAI API or Claude API (optional, for personalisation) - CRM: Notion, Airtable, or HubSpot (free) BLUEPRINT STEPS: STEP 1 — CAPTURE → Website form submits to Make.com trigger → Make.com creates contact in CRM → Tags lead with source (where they came from) STEP 2 — QUALIFY (AI-powered, optional) → Make.com sends lead data to OpenAI API → Prompt: "Based on this lead's job title and company size, score them 1-10 for likelihood to buy [YOUR OFFER]. Return: score, reasoning, recommended next action." → Store score in CRM field STEP 3 — NURTURE SEQUENCE → Trigger email sequence based on lead source: - Cold lead (score 1-5): 5-email educational sequence over 2 weeks - Warm lead (score 6-8): 3-email offer sequence over 5 days - Hot lead (score 9-10): immediate sales notification to you + personal email STEP 4 — ENGAGEMENT TRACKING → Tag leads who open emails / click links → When lead opens 3+ emails OR clicks a CTA: → Trigger "hot lead" Slack/SMS notification to you → Move lead to "Ready to buy" CRM stage → Start sales sequence STEP 5 — CLOSE THE LOOP → If lead books a call → mark in CRM → stop nurture sequence → If lead buys → add to onboarding sequence → remove from nurture → If 30 days no engagement → move to "cold" → add to re-engagement list EXPECTED RESULTS: - 0 manual follow-up emails - Leads contacted within 5 minutes of signing up - 20-30% increase in qualified call bookings
💡 Blueprint tip: Start with Steps 1-3 using free tools before adding AI scoring. Get the sequence working manually for 2 weeks, then automate based on what you observe.
